Intro

Nearly derived from Team Team Prime formed in 2014, Succmore was founded December of 2017 by Jake, Conner, and Ryan dragging Jared into their group chat. Since then, the Pokemaster Kenny from TTP has since made his own team Pladexmon (you can check him out here ). Early 2018, James joined the team, and ever since we've been traveling as a team to tournaments across the state.

Our intention as a team has always been to spread our knowledge of the realm of the TCG to everyone across the globe. To this day, we've been providing quality content including deck profiles, tournament reports, and metagame analyses.

You can check us out at our Facebook, where we post everything mentioned above.

We're more than happy to answer any questions, give deck advice, or anything else you have on your mind. You can either shoot us a Facebook message by clicking Send Message at the link, or send us an email.

the team

Succmore is only equipped with the most elite Pokemon TCG players in the nation. All players have topped and won numerous events nationwide including League Cups, Regional Championships, and the Packala Open.

jared "rukariosan" grimes

Top 32 Fort Wayne Regionals 2016, 6 League Cup Tops from 2017-2018, 2nd at Michigan ARG States 2018, won two win-a-boxes, two time Packala Open Champion, and wrote hit single "Bulu Gang".

ryan "scizor" shore

Almost made Day 2 of North American Internationals 2017, Packala Open 2015 runner up, topped decked an insane Guzma, and low key came up with Rainbow Road at Nationals 2016.

jake "reeee" bragdon

Plays Espeon Garb at everything, topped 6 City Championships with Machamp Ariados, premier writer for Succ Deck Sunday.

conner "slow boi" click

Won a league cup in 2018, topped 3 Cities with Marowak BREAK, plays Solrock and Lunatone in his Zoroark decks, the slowest player you'll ever play against, and wears his Northville Sousa sweatshirt to scare off opponents.

james "bulu" long

Plays Bulu at every event ever, the godmaster of pulls (RR Charizard, Ultra Ball, FA Lele) out of minimal packs, is going to play Ho-Oh/Charizard-GX at the first expanded event he goes to.
